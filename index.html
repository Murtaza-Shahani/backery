About Us

Welcome to Rains Strata Bakery,
where every bite is a journey through time and flavor. ains Bakery is a family-owned and operated bakery that has been baking delicious pastries and breads since 1995. We are located in the heart of downtown Fort William, and we are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality baked goods.
OUR STORY
Rains Bakery was founded in 1995 by Kira Weston, a passionate baker with a love of all things sweet. Kira started the bakery out of her home kitchen, and quickly gained a reputation for her delicious pastries and breads. In 2000, Kira moved the bakery to its current location in downtown Fort William. The bakery has since expanded to include a cafe, where customers can enjoy their pastries and coffee in a relaxing atmosphere. Rains Bakery is now a beloved institution in Fort William, and it continues to be a family-owned and operated business
